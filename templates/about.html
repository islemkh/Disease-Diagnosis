Internet Medical Diagnosis Engine

Everyone has a moment where they feel a twinge, or feel like they are coming down with something, but are not sure if it warrants a call to the doctor, or a trip to the emergency room. This usually results in a trip down the internet "rabbit-hole" in search of answers. We then waste hours or days convincing ourselves that we're dying of some obscure tropical disease, when we really just have a common cold. This application seeks to solve that problem. Using the Disease Diagnosis, we walk you through an intelligent interview process to provide you with the most likely possible diagnoses, and suggestions on next steps.
